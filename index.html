Process

How Split Pay works

01

Resident applies and gets approved

Takes a few minutes. No landlord involvement required.

02

They receive an ACH account and routing number

A dedicated virtual account unique to each resident.

03

They enter that account into your existing portal

Works with any resident portal that accepts ACH. In Plaid-supported portals, residents can search "Rent App" directly.

04

You receive full rent through your PMS — as normal

No difference in how or when you receive payment.

05

We handle underwriting, servicing, and resident support

All resident communication and servicing is on us.

06

Residents build credit automatically

Every on-time payment is reported to Equifax and Experian — free, with no action required from you or the resident.

07

Not fully approved? Approved for Less.

Residents who don't qualify for a full 50% split can start with a smaller portion — say 20–30% — and earn higher amounts over time.

To your system, Split Pay is just another bank account.
