What works best

Time it around rent week
The best time to send is 3–5 days before the 1st. Residents are already thinking about rent. Urgency is natural, not forced.
Impact
🌍
Use Spanish & Portuguese too
The flyer comes in EN, ES & PT. If your building has non-English speaking residents, sending in their language can 2× your sign-up rate for that group.
Impact

New residents are the most receptive — they're already setting everything up. Including Split Pay in your welcome materials takes zero ongoing effort.
Impact

Train your leasing team

Watch out for
Do's and don'ts
✅ Do this
→ Keep the message short and benefit-focused
→ Say it's free for them to try (no contract)
→ Mention the 5-minute application
→ Make clear nothing changes in their portal
→ Follow up — once is rarely enough
❌ Avoid this
→ Don't oversell — just inform, residents decide
→ Don't say Split Pay "guarantees" or "covers" rent
→ Don't say all residents will qualify (~50% do)
→ Don't bury the link — make rent.app/go prominent
→ Don't forget to send it to new move-ins
